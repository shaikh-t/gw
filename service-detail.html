About This Service

The UAE Golden Visa offers long-term residency for investors, entrepreneurs, specialized talents, and outstanding students — without the need for a local sponsor. GlobalWays connects you with verified vendors who handle eligibility assessment, filing, biometrics, and follow-through until approval.
